## **2026-06-25**

**RPC — `aster_getBalance` New Staking Fields**

- Updated `POST /info` (`aster_getBalance`) in RPC documentation (EN & ZH):
  - Added `staking` object to the response example and field descriptions.
  - New fields:
    - `staking.totalStakedAmount` (STRING) — Total staked ASTER.
    - `staking.totalUnclaimedRewards` (ARRAY) — Unclaimed rewards; each item has `asset` and `amount`.
    - `staking.totalPendingStakeAmount` (STRING) — Total pending stake amount.
    - `staking.totalPendingUnstakeAmount` (STRING) — Total pending unstake amount.
  - Note: a field returns `null` when its amount is 0; the entire `staking` block is omitted under privacy mode.
